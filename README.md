Abstract
ØThe “Smart Crop Disease Diagnosis Portal (CropCare)” is a community-oriented digital solution designed to help farmers identify crop diseases early and receive instant treatment guidance in simple Telugu and English.
ØThe portal provides symptom-based disease diagnosis, treatment steps, voice assistance, and an expert directory.
ØIt was developed after field visits, farmer interactions, surveys, and real-time observations in Gudlavalleru village.
ØThe project aims to reduce crop loss, improve pesticide usage practices, and make disease diagnosis simple, fast, and accessible.
﻿
Objectives
To help farmers recognize early symptoms of crop diseases.
To create awareness about safe pesticide usage and alternative organic treatments.
To teach farmers how to use the CropCare portal for disease diagnosis.
To reduce crop loss through timely and accurate treatment suggestions.
To enable farmers to access expert help through a digital platform.
To promote sustainable farming practices and digital literacy.
﻿
﻿Scope
Usable for rural and semi-literate farmers.
Supports multiple crops: paddy, tomato, chili, cotton, vegetables.
Multi-language: English & Telugu.
Provides voice-based interaction for convenience.
Works on mobile phones with simple UI.
Can be expanded with AI-based leaf-image detection, weather alerts, and offline mode in the future.
﻿
Proposed solution
A symptom-based disease diagnosis portal for farmer.
Supports English & Telugu.
Treatment suggestions—organic & chemical.
Contact directory of experts.
Treatment tracker & reminders.
Simple mobile-friendly interface﻿
﻿
Conclusion
The project identified key community problems and delivered a practical digital solution. 
CropCare enabled farmers to recognize diseases early, choose proper treatments, and reduce chemical misuse. 
Awareness programs and field testing improved their digital literacy. 
Overall, the portal supports sustainable farming and enhances crop health and farmer income.
